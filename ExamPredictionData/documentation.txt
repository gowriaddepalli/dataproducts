
ABOUT THIS APP:

This is shiny application which predicts your percentage as per your input.

The user panel of the lefthand side ask for the following inputs:
1)your Roll number: which is usually given to each student to particularly identify everyone uniquely and maximum of the students being 200.

2)your Invigilator/Invigilators: Names of teachers who are supposed to check your exam papers

3)The papers student gave where physics,biology and maths whose marks they were supposed to enter in the numeric input having minimmum 0,maximum 100 and a step size of 1.

4)The date field mentions the date of birth of the students.

The submit button sends the data to server.R,which processes the required data to give a predictive outcome,that is it calculates the marks of the student who appeared the three exams.

The display side,on the right side of the window,displays the student information as per entered and gives the predicted output.
